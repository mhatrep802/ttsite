The Future of PCB Design Education

TraceTutor is redefining how future engineers learn PCB design — making it intuitive, hands-on, and industry-ready from day one.

😫

The Problem

Engineering students are expected to design PCBs with almost no formal training. Most learn from scattered, tool-specific resources — with no structure or feedback.

🎯

The Solution

TraceTutor turns PCB design into a guided, gamified experience with real feedback and tool-agnostic training. Students graduate confident and industry-ready.

🧠

Product

Interactive modules, real-time scoring, tool-flexible challenges, and project-based learning. Built for students, scalable for schools and teams.

🚀

What's Unique

Not tool-locked. Real feedback. Designed like Codecademy, but for hardware. We fill the gaps left by Altium Academy and Fedevel.

📈

Traction

Pilot interest from universities. Feedback from professors. Students and forums expressing demand. Prototype built and user research ongoing.

💸

Business Model

Freemium for students. Premium tiers for schools and startups. Scalable via curriculum adoption and team training programs.

💰

Investment

Raising funds to finish our MVP, launch university pilots, and reach our first 1,000 users. Seeking experienced EdTech and SaaS investors.

🧑‍💻

Team

Led by a dual EE/CE student with hands-on PCB experience and a deep understanding of peer struggles. Backed by mentors and educators.

📣

Call to Action

Let's build the Codecademy of hardware. Visit TraceTutor or contact us to join our pilot, invest, or collaborate.
